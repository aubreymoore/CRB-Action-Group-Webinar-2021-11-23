CRB Action Group Webinar 3

November 23, 2021

The CRB Action Group is an ad hoc committee of entomologists and others working on the coconut rhinoceros beetle (CRB) on Pacific Islands. A particularly troublesome form of this pest, refered to as CRB-G, has recently invaded several islands, it is causing much damage, and it is spreading within the region.

This is the third in a series of webinars that the group has had since COVID impeded travel to conventional scientific meetings.

The organizers thank Bryan Blas of the University of Guam Office of Technology for all his help in setting up and assist with communications for this webinar.
